BLOOD DONATION
  • Blood donation is a voluntary process where individuals donate their blood to be used for patients in need of blood transfusions due to accidents, surgeries, or medical conditions like anemia or cancer.
  • Generally, individuals aged 17 to 65 and weighing at least 50 kg (110 lbs) are eligible to donate blood.
  • Blood is classified into different types: A, B, AB, and O. Each type can be either Rh-positive or Rh-negative.
  • Knowing one's blood type is important for both donors and recipients to ensure compatibility.
